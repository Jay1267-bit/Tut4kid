8th Grade ELA

Curriculum

In 8th grade ELA students engage with complex texts and analyze how authors use rhetoric, structure, and literary devices. Writing includes crafting arguments, research projects, and literary analysis with strong evidence and clarity.

Price

All courses are 100% free for students because its a non-profit organization. This helps everyone learn no matter the price.

Where It Takes Place

Classes happen online through Zoom meetings. The students are going to be informed with the class timings after signing up.
