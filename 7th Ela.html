7th Grade ELA

Curriculum

In 7th grade ELA students evaluate how authors develop ideas and characters and compare different texts and media. Writing emphasizes clear organization, strong thesis statements, and the use of relevant textual evidence.

Price

All courses are 100% free for students because its a non-profit organization. This helps everyone learn no matter the price.

Where It Takes Place
