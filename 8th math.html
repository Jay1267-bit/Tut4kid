8th Grade Math

Curriculum

8th grade math focuses on linear equations, functions, and systems of equations. Students also explore geometry concepts like the Pythagorean Theorem and transformations on the coordinate plane.

Price

All courses are 100% free for students because its a non-profit organization. This helps everyone learn no matter the price.

Where It Takes Place
