7th Grade Math

Curriculum

7th grade math strengthens proportional reasoning and introduces solving multi-step equations and inequalities. Students also work with probability, statistics, and operations with rational numbers, including negative fractions and decimals.

Price

All courses are 100% free for students because its a non-profit organization. This helps everyone learn no matter the price.

Where It Takes Place

Classes happen online through Zoom meetings. The students are going to be informed with the class timings after signing up.
