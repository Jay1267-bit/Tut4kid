5th Grade ELA

Curriculum

In 5th grade ELA students explore a range of literature, including stories, poems, and informational texts, to build comprehension and vocabulary. They begin to write more structured essays, focusing on opinion, informative, and narrative styles.

Price

All courses are 100% free for students because its a non-profit organization. This helps everyone learn no matter the price.

Where It Takes Place

Classes happen online through Zoom meetings. The students are going to be informed with the class timings after signing up.
